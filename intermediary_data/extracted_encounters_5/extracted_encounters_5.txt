Folder: extracted_encounters_5
Description: 
Contains participant data extracted from the raw data. 
Data was extracted by finding the timestamp where the driver vehicle was closest to the intersection locations for their driving route. 
The encounter was then extracted using this timestamp as the end of the encounter, and using an encounter duration of 5 secounds.
Encounters were extracted from the raw files into gaze, imu and driving data csv files, and each are separated into their own participant folder

Parameters used:
encounter_size = 5 seconds
